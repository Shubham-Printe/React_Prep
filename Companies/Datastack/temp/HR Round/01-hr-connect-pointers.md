## HR Connect — Pointers to Keep in Mind (Datastack)

### Goals for the call
- **Get clarity**: role scope + level/title + team + expectations.
- **Lock logistics**: location/remote, start date, notice period, work authorization.
- **Close the loop**: compensation + benefits + offer process/timeline.

### Role & level alignment
- **Title/Level**: Senior React Developer — confirm official title and what it maps to internally.
- **Team & manager**: which team, who you report to, hiring manager’s name.
- **Scope**: JD mentions developing UI components, Redux/Flux workflows, reusable components, state management, API integration, code reviews, and **providing guidance to other React developers** with **aspiration to lead the entire React developer team**. Confirm day-to-day mix (hands-on vs mentoring vs standards).
- **Success criteria**: what “good in 30/60/90 days” looks like.
  - **Ask HR**: “How do you define success for this role in the first 30/60/90 days, and how will it be evaluated?”
- **Work model**: **Location: Wakad**. Confirm if fully on-site, hybrid, or flexible; expected days in office and any flexibility.

### Compensation (ask for the full breakdown)
- **Base salary**: range + final target.
- **Bonus**: target %, payout schedule, performance criteria.
- **CTC structure**: fixed vs variable, allowances, deductions.
- **Ask HR**: “Can you share the full CTC breakup with fixed vs variable, allowances, and expected in-hand?”
- **Revision cycle**: when the first appraisal/review happens.

### Benefits & policies
- **Health insurance**: coverage for self/spouse/parents, waiting period.
- **PTO/holidays**: annual leaves, sick leave, carry-forward/encashment.
- **Work hours**: flexibility, core hours, overtime/comp-off.
- **Equipment**: laptop, WFH allowance, internet reimbursement.
- **Learning budget**: courses/certifications/conferences.
- **Notice period**: theirs and yours, buyout options.

### Offer process & next steps
- **Timeline**: when to expect the written offer and decision deadline.
- **Rounds left**: any additional rounds (e.g. technical L2, bar-raiser).
- **Documentation**: payslips, offer letters, ID, education, etc.

### Negotiation hygiene
- Ask for **written details** before committing verbally.
- Share expectations as a **range** (not a single number), and anchor to the **complete package**.
- Clarify constraints: notice period, competing timelines, flexibility on joining.

### Red flags to watch for
- Vague role/team (“we’ll decide later”).
- Unclear variable pay rules.
- Pressure to accept quickly without written offer.
- Mismatch between interview scope and HR-stated expectations/level.
