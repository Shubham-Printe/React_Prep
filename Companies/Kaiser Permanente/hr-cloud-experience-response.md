# How to answer: "Do you have cloud experience?"

Use one of the options below depending on how the HR asked (call vs email) and how much detail you want to give.

---

## Option A – Short (for a quick call or chat)

**"I have experience with cloud-based deployment—for example hosting frontends on Vercel—and with CI/CD pipelines as part of our release process. I haven’t worked directly on AWS or Azure infrastructure, but I’m comfortable with deployment practices and can pick up the cloud stack with onboarding support."**

---

## Option B – Slightly more detail (if she asks for clarification)

**"On the cloud side, I’ve used platforms like Vercel for frontend hosting and have worked with CI/CD as part of our release and deployment process. My hands-on experience is more on the application and deployment side rather than on setting up cloud infrastructure (e.g. AWS/Azure). I’m open to learning the specific cloud tools Kaiser Permanente uses and would rely on initial onboarding or training if the role expects it."**

---

## Option C – One-line (if she just needs a yes/no style answer)

**"I have exposure to cloud deployment and CI/CD rather than deep infrastructure; I’m willing to learn the specific cloud stack with support."**

---

## What you can truthfully lean on

- **Vercel** – cloud hosting (your portfolio is on Vercel).
- **CI/CD** – listed on your resume; part of release/deployment.
- **Enterprise release and deployment practices** – from your current role.
- **Willingness to learn** – especially for a Web UI/UX role where cloud may be supporting, not central.

Use Option A for most calls; use B if she probes; use C for a very brief reply (e.g. WhatsApp/text).
