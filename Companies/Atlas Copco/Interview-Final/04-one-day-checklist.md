# Final Round — One-Day Checklist (Technical + HR + Negotiation)

Use this the **night before** and **morning of** your final round. Covers **technical**, **behavioural/HR**, and **negotiation**.

---

## Tonight (45–60 min)

### Technical
- [ ] Rehearse **intro** (60–90 sec) out loud — `01-technical-prep.md` §2.
- [ ] Rehearse **Story 1** (MVP rescue) and **Story 2** (context menu) — bullets only; 5 min each.
- [ ] Skim **React one-page cheat** and **coding quick ref** — `01-technical-prep.md` §4, §5.
- [ ] Run through **one DSA** (e.g. Two Sum) and **debounce** (paper or editor) — full code in `../Interview-L2/02-level2-coding-practice-ideal-answers.md`.

### HR / Behavioural
- [ ] Rehearse **“Why leaving?”** and **“Why Atlas Copco?”** — `02-hr-behavioural-prep.md` §2.
- [ ] Rehearse **strength** and **weakness** (with example and what you’re doing about it).
- [ ] Pick **3–4 questions to ask** (role, team, growth, next steps) — `02-hr-behavioural-prep.md` §3.
- [ ] Skim **STAR** and one conflict/failure example so you can adapt on the spot.

### Negotiation
- [ ] Fill in **your numbers** in `03-negotiation-prep.md` §2 (min/target fixed, notice period).
- [ ] Read **scripts** (§4) and **must-ask clarifiers** (§5); know your preference (sign-on > variable).
- [ ] Decide one line for **“What are your salary expectations?”** (range or “what range do you have?”).

### Logistics
- [ ] Confirm **time, link, and format** (video/call, who’s on the call).
- [ ] Quiet space, device charged, backup internet if possible.

---

## Morning of (15–20 min)

### Technical
- [ ] Re-read **opening questions** and **intro** — `01-technical-prep.md` §1, §2.
- [ ] Quick pass on **two stories** (bullets) and **company one-liners** — §3, §6.

### HR / Negotiation
- [ ] Re-read **why leave / why Atlas Copco** and your **salary line** — `02-hr-behavioural-prep.md` §2, `03-negotiation-prep.md` §4.
- [ ] Glance at **questions to ask** and **must-ask clarifiers** — `02-hr-behavioural-prep.md` §3, `03-negotiation-prep.md` §5.

### Mindset
- [ ] Breathe. You’ve prepared. Be clear, honest, and professional.
