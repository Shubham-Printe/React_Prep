# Atlas Copco — Final Round: Technical + HR/Negotiation

This folder contains **full preparation** for the final round: **technical** (coding, React, stories) and **HR/behavioural + negotiation**.

## Round format

- **Technical:** Coding (DSA / frontend), React depth, design/architecture, your delivery/debugging stories.
- **HR / Behavioural:** Experience, motivation, teamwork, conflict, strengths/weaknesses, why Atlas Copco, why leave.
- **Negotiation:** Compensation (CTC, fixed/variable, sign-on), role/level, notice period, benefits, offer timeline.
