# Resignation exit checklist & ideal timeline

Use this after you have **tendered resignation** (India / typical IT employer). Confirm specifics with your **HR policy** and **appointment letter**.

---

## Timeline at a glance

| Phase | When | Focus |
|-------|------|--------|
| **A** | Week 1 of notice | Written acknowledgement, LWD, exit process, leave, PF/UAN, F&F rules |
| **B** | Middle of notice period | Handover, assets, payslips, insurance end date |
| **C** | Last 1–2 weeks before LWD | Relieving / experience letter process, no-dues |
| **D** | Last working day (LWD) | Return assets, clearance, final pay clarity |
| **E** | ~30–45 days after LWD (typical) | F&F, Form 16, PF transfer/withdrawal, chase letters if missing |

---

## Phase A — Week 1 (right after resignation)

| # | Item | Why | What to do |
|---|------|-----|------------|
| A1 | **Resignation acknowledged in writing** | Proof of notice start | Ask manager/HR for email confirming **receipt**, **notice start date**, and **last working day** once fixed. |
| A2 | **Last working day (LWD)** | Avoid disputes | Ensure **LWD is confirmed in email** (not only verbal). |
| A3 | **Exit / clearance process** | Know the path | Ask HR: **clearance form**, **asset return**, **handover sign-off**, **who approves** each step. |
| A4 | **Full & final (F&F)** | Cash flow | Ask: **salary** for notice months, **when F&F settles**, **typical TAT** after LWD. |
| A5 | **Leave balance** | Encashment / liability | **Balance**, **encashment** rules, **deadline** to apply or use. |
| A6 | **PF / UAN** | Continuity | Confirm **UAN** and **KYC**; ask **PF transfer** vs **withdrawal** process (often online). |
| A7 | **Form 16 / tax** | Next employer / ITR | **When** Form 16 for FY; if **mid-year exit**, how **TDS** and **investment proofs** are handled. |
| A8 | **Salary slips** | Next job / loans | **Save each month’s slip** until LWD (download from portal if available). |
| A9 | **Group health insurance** | Gap until next employer | **Coverage end date**; **portability / COBRA** if offered. |

**Optional — email HR (week 1):**  
*“Please share the exit checklist — clearance, relieving & experience letter timeline, F&F and Form 16, and asset return — for my records.”*

---

## Phase B — During notice (middle weeks)

| # | Item | Why | What to do |
|---|------|-----|------------|
| B1 | **Handover** | Clean exit + reference | **Written handover** (ownership, repos, access, runbooks); **KT sessions**; **sign-off** if company uses it. |
| B2 | **Company assets** | No false liability | List **laptop, ID card, dongles, keys**; confirm **return date** (usually LWD). |
| B3 | **Access (repos, admin, tools)** | Security / clarity | Ask **when** access is revoked (often LWD EOD). **Do not** take IP; export only what policy allows. |
| B4 | **Personal data on devices** | Privacy | Move **personal files** off laptop; clear **personal browser sessions** if permitted. |
| B5 | **LinkedIn / public profile** | Professionalism | Update **after LWD** or as per comfort/policy if early exit creates friction. |
| B6 | **Reference** | Future roles | If relationship allows, ask if manager/HR ok as **reference** and **contact**. |

---

## Phase C — Last 1–2 weeks before LWD

| # | Item | Why | What to do |
|---|------|-----|------------|
| C1 | **Experience / service letter** | Often required by next employer | Ask **when** issued — **on LWD** vs **after clearance**; confirm **company legal name** and **dates**. |
| C2 | **Relieving letter** | Proof of exit | Same — **timeline**, **signatory**, **soft copy** availability. |
| C3 | **No-dues / clearance** | Unblocks letters & F&F | Know **all departments** (IT, admin, finance, HR) and **order** of sign-off. |

---

## Phase D — Last working day (LWD)

| # | Item | Why | What to do |
|---|------|-----|------------|
| D1 | **Return assets** | Clearance | Hand back **laptop, ID, etc.**; get **receipt** or **email confirmation** if possible. |
| D2 | **Exit interview** | Optional | Stay **polite and factual**; **do not sign** documents you disagree with; ask for **copy** of any form you sign. |
| D3 | **Final payslip** | Records | Confirm **last month** pay and **leave encashment** line items when slip is published. |
| D4 | **Company email** | Continuity | Note **what happens** to mailbox after LWD (forwarding rules if any). |

---

## Phase E — After LWD (~30–45 days typical; varies by company)

| # | Item | Why | What to do |
|---|------|-----|------------|
| E1 | **F&F settlement** | Dues | **Track** payment; if delayed, **escalate HR** with policy TAT. |
| E2 | **Form 16** | ITR | **Download** when issued (sometimes only after F&F). |
| E3 | **PF** | Continuity | Complete **transfer to new employer PF** or **withdrawal** per EPFO rules and your choice. |
| E4 | **Relieving / experience (if not on LWD)** | Next employer | **Follow up** until you have **PDFs**; store safely (personal drive + next employer onboarding). |

---

## Next employer (e.g. Atlas Copco)

- Keep **relieving letter**, **experience letter**, and **recent payslips** ready for **onboarding / BGV** if requested again.
- Once **LWD** is fixed, **confirm joining date** with HR in **writing**.
