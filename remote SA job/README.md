# Remote salary (international) job — notes

Notes for comparing **Indian employer packages** (LPA, PF, gratuity, TDS) with **international / remote-first employers** that pay in **USD or via global payroll**.

**Not legal or tax advice.** Rules change; confirm with a **Chartered Accountant** for your exact residency, contract, and income type.

## Quick mental model

- **Indian company:** “CTC” bundles salary + retirals + sometimes variable; **monthly in-hand** is after TDS, PF, PT, etc. You’re used to this.
- **Foreign company:** You almost never get the same bundle with the same labels. What matters is **(1) who employs you**, **(2) currency and gross**, **(3) statutory deductions / taxes in India**, **(4) benefits you must buy yourself** if you’re not on Indian payroll.
