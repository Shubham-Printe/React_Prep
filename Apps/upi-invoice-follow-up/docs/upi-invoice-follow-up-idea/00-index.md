# Idea 1 — UPI Invoice + Payment Follow‑up

## Goal

Get your **first payout** fast by building a small web app that helps freelancers/agencies:
- generate a clean invoice + share link/PDF
- attach UPI payment instructions (QR + UPI ID)
- track paid/unpaid
- send follow-ups without awkwardness (copy/paste templates first)

## Why this is the first app

- Clear pain: **getting paid late**
- Easy to demo in 2 minutes
- One-time pricing works well for a first win
- India-friendly payments (Razorpay/Cashfree) when you’re ready
